How to play Simon:

Objective: correctly repeat a longer and longer sequence of signals

  • A one player game
  • You will be instructed to observe Simon's moves
  • Simon will click on different color buttons in a specific sequence
  • Then, you'll have to replicate the sequence accurately to move on, so pay close attention!
  • The levels will increase in difficulty the higher you go

    • GOOD LUCK!
